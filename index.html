Frequently Asked Questions

Can a notary public give me legal advice?
I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW IN TEXAS AND MAY NOT GIVE LEGAL ADVICE OR ACCEPT FEES FOR LEGAL ADVICE.
Do you offer after hours services?
Absolutely, please text us at 832-761-3663,l or email us for after hours or other special requests! Note that additional fees apply.
Can you perform notary acts in all Texas counties?
Yes, all active Texas commissioned notary publics can perform notary acts anywhere in the state of Texas.
Will I need a government issued ID?
Yes. You will need a valid, unexpired government-issued photo identification. The name on the ID must reasonably match the name on the document.
What are the requirements for notarization?
  1. The signer must be positively identified by the Notary.
  2. The signer must personally and willingly appear before the Notary.
  3. The Notary must perform the act on the date stated on the certificate.
