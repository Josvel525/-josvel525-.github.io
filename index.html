Frequently Asked Questions
Can a notary public give me legal advice? I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW IN TEXAS AND MAY NOT GIVE LEGAL ADVICE OR ACCEPT FEES FOR LEGAL ADVICE.
Do you offer after hours services? Absolutely, please text us at 832-356-0671 or email info@texaspublicnotary.us for after-hours or other special requests.
Can Texas notaries serve in all counties? Yes, all commissioned Texas notaries may perform notarial acts anywhere in the state of Texas.
Do I need government-issued ID to be notarized? Yes. The ID must be valid and the name must match the document.
Can you notarize documents already signed? Yes, as long as each signer is physically present with valid ID at the time of notarization.
What else is required for notarization?
  1. The signer must be positively identified.
  2. The signer must appear willingly before the notary.
  3. The notarial act must occur on the date stated in the certificate.
